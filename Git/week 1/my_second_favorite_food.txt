Ingredients* 100g/3?oz organic beef fillet, finely chopped* 2 gherkins, finely chopped* 1 tsp capers, rinsed and drained, finely chopped* 1 shallot, finely chopped* ? tsp Dijon mustard* few drops Tabasco sauce* ? tsp Worcestershire sauce* salt and freshly ground black pepper* 1 slice dark rye or pumpernickel bread* 1 free-range egg yolkMethod1. Place the chopped beef, gherkins, capers and shallot into a bowl and mix well.2. Add the mustard, Tabasco sauce, Worcestershire sauce, salt and freshly ground black pepper and mix well.3. To serve, spoon the steak tartare mixture onto a slice of plain or toasted dark rye or pumpernickel bread.4. Make a small dent in the top of the tartare and top with a fresh egg yolk. Eat immediately.
